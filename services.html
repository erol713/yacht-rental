  • Yacht Management

    Kuzmanic Yacht Charter is recognized throughout the industry for personal service, transparency, commitment to quality and forward thinking. While other companies have fallen victim to global economic changes, Kuzmanic Yacht Charter Management has gone for strength to strength.

  • Charter Management

    Consider the financial benefits of buying and including your yacht in our charter fleet in order to reduce the cost of ownership. The basic idea is to offer you the possibility to buy a new yacht subject to the smallest possible investment. This way owning a yacht becomes a pleasure as we handle the maintenance of the yacht, management of the crew, accounts and supplies, with your yacht being prepared for your own sailing use as well.

  • Yacht Brokerage

    Our company has specialized in yacht brokerage, in the mediation of yacht sale and purchase. We provide the list of all the yachts for sale and purchase, from the small boats to big luxury yachts, both sailing and motor yachts; with complete specification for each yacht, photos and technical details. Once you have established which yacht fits your needs, our company will provide the full service strategy in the boat market or find the best suitable alternative.
